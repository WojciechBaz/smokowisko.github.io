Virtual Dragon Adoption Program

In order to support the noble mission and statutory goals of Smokowisko National Dragon Park, the Directors and devoted Custodians of the park have introduced a unique opportunity for friends of nature: the virtual adoption of one of our cherished inhabitants. By becoming a Patron of a Dragon, you join a tradition that stretches back to the park’s founding, playing a direct role in safeguarding their future.

The idea of patronage is not new to Smokowisko. In 1894, Countess Elżbieta Dąbrowska, famed for her philanthropic work in preserving wild habitats, became the first honorary patron of a young silver-scaled dragon. In that same year, composer Aleksander Siennicki dedicated part of his concert tour earnings to improving dragon nesting grounds, while explorer Jan Franciszek Orłowski brought rare herbs from the Tatra mountains to aid dragon health. In modern times, organizations such as the Polish Nature Alliance, Green Future Foundation, and the International Conservation Trust have lent their generous support to this initiative.

Becoming a Patron is open to anyone between the ages of 8 and 100, or younger with the consent of a legal guardian. We look for Patrons who embody the finest qualities: courage to stand for what is right, kindness toward all living creatures, curiosity to learn, and the wisdom to care for something beyond oneself. As a Patron, you inspire others and set an example of stewardship for future generations.
