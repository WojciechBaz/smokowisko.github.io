Our Adoption Program

Virtual adoption lets you become a patron of Smokowisko’s dragons—funding habitat protection, research and education.

How It Works

To strengthen funding for conservation under the Park’s charter, the Custodians and staff opened a virtual adoption program. Patrons symbolically adopt a monitored dragon (e.g., Iskra, Żar, or Mira) and receive seasonal updates, photos, and invitations to learning events. Adoptions are open to patrons aged 8–100 (under 18s require a legal guardian).

We especially encourage qualities like bravery, kindness, curiosity, patience, and respect for nature. Dragon knowledge helps—but a good heart matters most.

Reminder: Park access is limited to marked viewpoints and trails. Dragon encounters are never guaranteed; we observe from a distance to keep them safe.

Adoption Application

Applicants under 18 must add a guardian below.

By submitting you agree that your details may be used to process your adoption and to contact you with Park updates. We never share your data outside the Park.

Required: Application Video

After submitting the form, please record a short application video telling us why you should be chosen as a dragon patron. Share it via the Smokowisko WhatsApp channel (button below).

What to include

  • Introduce yourself (first name is enough; add age if you like).
  • Why you? Tell us about qualities that fit a patron: brave, kind, smart, curious, patient, respectful of wildlife.
  • Dragon knowledge: one or two facts you learned (species, behavior, safety rules).
  • Your promise: how you’ll help protect dragons and follow Park rules.

Technical guidelines

  • Length: 60–120 seconds. Shoot horizontally if possible.
  • Title the video: Smokowisko_Adoption_[YourName]_[ChosenDragon].mp4
  • For applicants under 18: record with a guardian’s consent, and have your guardian appear or confirm at the end.
