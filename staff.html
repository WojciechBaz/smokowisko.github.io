Our Most Notable Staff Members

Jan Kowalski – Head Ranger

With over 30 years patrolling the Smokowisko Forest, Jan is the Park’s living encyclopedia. Known for his calm approach even when spotting a Draconis sylvanus up close, he is responsible for ranger training and safety protocols.

Magdalena Wiśniewska – Senior Dragon Biologist

Magdalena leads the Park’s dragon behavior research unit. She specializes in scale iridescence studies and migratory path mapping, and has authored several field manuals for dragon observation.

Piotr Nowak – Cultural Liaison

Tasked with preserving the local folklore that surrounds dragons, Piotr bridges modern conservation with centuries-old traditions. He organizes seasonal festivals and educational programs for visitors.
