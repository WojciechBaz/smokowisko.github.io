About the Park

Hidden deep in the forests of southern Poland, Smokowisko National Dragon Park is home to some of the last remaining dragons in Europe. These majestic creatures live in harmony with the lush environment, protected and studied by our dedicated team of caretakers and researchers.

Meet Our Dragons

Iskra is a young female Polish Forest Dragon known for her shimmering silver-green scales and playful spirit. She loves cloudberries and flying through misty valleys at dawn.

Adopt a Dragon

Support conservation efforts and become part of our dragon family. Adopting a dragon like Iskra helps us preserve their natural habitat and fund vital research.

Each adoption includes a personalized certificate, dragon profile, photos, and a magical keepsake.
